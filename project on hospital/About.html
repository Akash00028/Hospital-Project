Welcome To N.S.C.H. SHEIKHPURA

NSCH SHEIKHPURA is the vision of the Honâ€™ble ex-Chief minister LALU PRASAD . the mandate to provide the most modern healthcare to the last man in the queue at affordable cost.

NSCH SHEIKHPURA was created by the ordinance dated 16th July, 2001 and then the Act (Amendment), 2001 on 2nd July, 2002.

In all, NSCH have been created and taken care by the PMSSY (Prime Minister Swasthya Surakhsa Yojana).

The work started in 2010 with construction in six packages by the Project Monitoring Unit of the MoHFW and is referred to as NSCH SHEIKHPURA project. The project management at individual sites was done by a Superintending Engineer on behalf of the MoHFW, the DPR consultant, the project consultant and the contractors till date.

Recruitment of faculty for all NSCH was undertaken by the MoHFW being coordinated by NSCH SHEIKHPURA from November, 2000 which processed six thousand five hundred online applications for 546 posts in by September 30th 2002. The institute is also in the process of recruiting more faculty and starting new departments.

The Hospital is well developed now, catering to approximately 1000 outpatients per day, with each department having its own OPD block area, running 3 major state of the art semi-modular operation theaters, and two minor operation theaters, in all performing more than 150 surgeries per month. It runs round the clock laboratory and has world class radiology with CT, MRI, Biplane DSA, Digital mammography, digital radiography, ultrasound with color doppler facilities.

The institute also has a telemedicine programme called Chhattisa, a community outreach programme which has conducted more than 100 health camps
