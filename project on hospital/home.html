Welcome to AIIMS Nishant Kumar Nirala

AIIMS Rishikesh is one of the SIX Apex healthcare institutes being established by the Ministry of Health & Family Welfare, Government of India under the Pradhan Mantri Swasthya Suraksha Yojna (PMSSY).
With the aim of correcting regional imbalances in quality tertiary level healthcare in the country and attaining self sufficiency in graduate and postgraduate medical education and training, the PMSSY planned to set up 6 new AIIMS institutions in under served areas of the country.
These institutions are being established by an Act of Parliament on the lines of the original All India Institute of Medical Sciences,New Delhi which imparts both undergraduate and postgraduate medical education in all its branches and related fields.

From The Director's Desk

A warm welcome from All India Institute of Medical Sciences, Patna

All India Institute of Medical Sciences was established as an institution of national importance by an Act of Parliament with the object to develop medical workforce to demonstrate a high standard of Medical Education and sofisticated health care at an affordable cost in India.
The Institute is developing as not only an excellent ‘Centre of Learning’ but is also establishing itself for the delivery of quality healthcare services. The main objectives of the Institute and my priorities are:
To develop a pattern of teaching in undergraduate and postgraduate education to demonstrate high standard of medical education for all medical colleges and other allied institutions in India.
To bring educational facilities of the highest order together in one place for the training of the personnel in all important branches of the health activity.
To attain self-sufficiency in postgraduate medical education.
When it comes to education, it is necessary to select the best institute to strengthen the career growth of your children. We, aim to be a centre of excellence both in the practice of medicine and training; we prepare enthusiastic and talented young people to play a key role at the forefront of their chosen profession. Through technical knowledge acquired from experienced and dedicated staff, the Institute has been setup with most modern equipment and infrastructure, which gives you the opportunities to be exceptional. Our strong commitment to “quality” is evident in our state of art infrastructure and the excellence of eminent consultants in all departments of the hospital, all these at an affordable cost. Our dedicated healthcare professional provides high quality personalized care. This Institution believes that Hospital and Doctors are there for the sake of healing. As always, we are looking to improve our care and service, I want to invite you to take a closer look at our services and tell us how can we improve?
I extend my deepest gratitude for your continuing support and confidence in our mission to provide quality health care and education.
